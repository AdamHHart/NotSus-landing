Why we exist

In today's world, children are bombarded with biased media and distractions competing for their attention. These influences can become unintended teachers, shaping young minds in unhealthy ways. NotSus.net exists to counteract this by providing a safe digital environment where children's screen time is productive and enriching.

How it works

NotSus.net is a browser designed specifically for kids, emphasizing productivity, curiosity, and creativity. It only grants access to carefully selected educational content and creative tools that help children develop knowledge, skills, individuality, and critical thinking.

By eliminating biased media and manipulative algorithms, NotSus.net gives authority back to children and their parents. The browser features user-friendly interfaces with engaging resources under "Learn" and "Create" menus, empowering kids to make choices aligned with their goals and values.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, empowering online platform that nurtures children's curiosity and creativity, free from biased influences, enabling them to develop valuable skills and a love for learning.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the digital experience for children by fostering a secure space where they can explore, create, and grow into knowledgeable, independent thinkers.
