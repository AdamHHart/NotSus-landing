Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26-September-2025
Last Updated: 02-December-2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NotSus ('we,' 'our,' or 'us'). We are committed to protecting the privacy of children, parents, and guardians who use our educational browsing platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in compliance with:

  • British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)
  • Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)
  • Principles similar to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide a safe and effective learning experience.

(a) Account Information
  • Name (child and parent)
  • Email address
  • Password or login credentials
(b) Usage & Activity Data
  • Websites visited through our safe browsing environment
  • Time spent on educational activities
  • Project history and progress
  • Interaction logs and analytics (to improve app performance)
(c) Device & Technical Data
  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser information
  • IP address (used only for security and analytics)
(d) Payment Information
  • Processed securely through third-party payment providers (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
  • We do not store credit card numbers or financial details directly

3. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to:

  • Operate and maintain user accounts
  • Provide parental dashboards and learning analytics
  • Monitor and filter online content for safety
  • Improve user experience and system performance
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Communicate important updates, security notices, or feature changes

4. Children's Privacy

We take special care to protect children's data:

  • Children under 13 must have parental or guardian consent before creating an account.
  • Parents may create accounts on behalf of their children or invite them under a family plan.
  • Parents can view, manage, or delete their child's data through the parental dashboard or by contacting us.
  • We do not display or share children's personal data publicly.

5. Parental Control & Consent

Parents/guardians can:

  • Review their child's activity reports
  • Update, correct, or delete account information
  • Withdraw consent and request account deletion at any time

To make such a request, contact us at: contact@notsus.net

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information.

We may share limited data only when necessary:

  • With service providers who help operate our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processors)
  • With educational platforms integrated into the app (e.g., Khan Academy, Scratch), to enable login or sync features
  • When required by law enforcement or regulatory authorities

Each third-party site has its own Privacy Policy. We recommend parents review these separately.

7. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access or misuse.

Examples include:

  • Encryption of transmitted data
  • Password hashing
  • Secure storage on Canada-based servers
  • Limited employee access to personal data

No system is 100% secure, but we follow industry standards to protect your data.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law.

When accounts are deleted, associated data will be securely erased or anonymized within 30 days.

9. Your Rights

Under PIPA and PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion of your or your child's data

Requests can be sent to: contact@notsus.net

10. Third-Party Websites

Our app provides access to external educational sites. These are operated independently and have their own privacy practices.

We are not responsible for content or privacy policies of those third-party websites.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If changes are significant, we will notify users via email or in-app notice before they take effect.

12. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or privacy-related requests, contact:

Email: contact@notsus.net
Address: NotSus.net, 2015 Main St. Vancouver, BC, Canada
